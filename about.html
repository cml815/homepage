About Caroline Leopold, MA, GPC

What I do.

Write and edit products worthy of funding or publication.

How it works.

Consult and then partner with you. You supply vision, expertise, and direction. And, I integrate expert input, data, and technical requirements into a cogent product. Revisions close the job.

Experience.

Since 1999, won $10 million from proposals. Background in community research, program evaluation, and program development. Have had good fortune to work continuously as a writer since 2005.

As a lifelong learner, I am constantly updating my skills. I am highly fluent in search, reference, and content technologies. In addition, I am a student in computer programming and aim to build web applications to assist researchers and non-profits compete for funding.

In addition to working for write-for-hire projects, I am also an author. I have authored news articles and editorials that have been published on websites in the US and the UK.
