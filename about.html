About Caroline Leopold, MA, GPC

What I do.

Write and edit products worthy of funding or publication.

How it works.

Consult and then partner with you. You supply vision, expertise, and direction. And, I integrate expert input, data, and technical requirements into a cogent product. Revisions always come with the job.

Experience.

Since 1999, I have won $10 million from proposals. My success is from expertise and understanding submission strategy. I only write grants where the return is worth the effort.

I am adept at seeing the big picture, while attending to minute details. Publications may fail on formatting alone. Grants are also strict. That is why systems (and backups) are critical.

In addition to working for write-for-hire projects, I also have bylines. I have authored news articles and editorials that have been published on websites.
